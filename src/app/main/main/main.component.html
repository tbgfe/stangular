In order to prove identity and foreign status when applying for an ITIN, the applicant needs to provide either a copy of a valid passport or a combination of two other documents.

All documents must be valid and current; expired documents will not be accepted.

If a copy of an applicant's passport provided as a supporting document, the copy must include the passport picture page and US visa page. If providing a combination of two other documents, one must prove the applicant's 'foreign status' and the other must prove their identity. All copies must be certified by the issuing agencies of the original document.
